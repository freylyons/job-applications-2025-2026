### Making Effective Decisions

In my current role, I resolved a critical issue of fragmented, manual data management that caused pervasive errors and required five hours of manual work weekly, creating significant business risk. My task was to design a new, automated system to ensure data integrity and provide a scalable data foundation.

After analysing the options, I rejected a prior monolithic approach as unscalable. Instead, I architected a new system around a central PostgreSQL database as a master data store. This decision was based on rigorous analysis of business needs; it decoupled ingestion from loading, created a single source of truth, and handled both automated and manual data feeds. This demonstrated objectivity, basing the recommendation on technical and business merit. I then built a multi-layer containerised Python pipeline to extract, validate, and load the data.

The result was the complete automation of the process. The new system is a reliable, daily service ensuring accuracy for over 10,000 product listings, mitigating business risk and establishing a trusted, evidence-based data foundation.
