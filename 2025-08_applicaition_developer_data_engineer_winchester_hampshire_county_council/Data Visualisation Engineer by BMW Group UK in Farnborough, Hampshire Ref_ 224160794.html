Job details

Salary Range: £40,643 - £45,077 per annum

Work Location: Elizabeth II Court, Winchester (Hybrid Working)

Hours per week: 37

Contract Type: Permanent

Closing Date: 12 September 2025

Contact Details for an Informal Chat About The Role: Lewis Childs, Integration Platforms Team Leader on lewis.childs@hants.gov.uk

Your role and team

As a member of our Integration Platforms Team, this role is a IT Development Consultant; developing, maintaining and supporting the corporate integration platform (Boomi), developing and maintaining processes built and executing upon it, including; interpreting business requirements, technical consultancy, delivering project artefacts, technical troubleshooting and service improvement.

You will also work as part of the wider Development and Applications team to support services and deliver technical resource to enable the initiatives and business of the broader County Council and its Partners.

The complexity, responsibility and accountability will be commensurate with the grade.

About you

It’s essential that you have the following:

Essential Qualification

  • Minimum of 2 A levels (grade A-C) or equivalent plus GCSEs (grade A-C) or equivalent including Maths and English
  • A degree in a related subject area and/or formal industry recognised qualifications/professional development of an equivalent level, or equivalent experience in a similar role

Essential Knowledge, Experience, Skills and Behaviours

  • Experience of working within a formal IT environment
  • IT/application support experience 1st, 2nd, or 3rd level within an enterprise-scale environment
  • Effective communication skills at all levels, including oral and written
  • Self-motivated and committed to on-going personal development
  • Able to establish effective working relationships with people at all levels
  • An ability to quickly absorb information and understand new technologies/solutions
  • Experience of working as part of a project team, delivering solutions on time and within budget

Desirable Qualifications, Knowledge, Experience, Skills and Behaviours

  • Practical knowledge of the Boomi platform, or similar enterprise iPaaS
  • Data Science techniques, data engineering, NLP, Python, data matching and analysis
  • Familiarity of JSON, XML, OData, SQL, JavaScript structures and syntax
  • Awareness of the role of an Enterprise Service Bus within a large organisation
  • Experience working with ETL or other integration tools
  • Formal IT Support qualifications
  • Knowledge of Local government applications and information workflows
  • ITIL working practices
  • Knowledge of development and project management tools and methodologies

Your responsibilities

In this role you will be responsible for:

Developing, Supporting and Maintaining the Corporate integration platform (Boomi) and its associated integrations:

  • 3rd Level support of services that the team is responsible for:
    • Fault diagnosis and resolution of incidents
    • Third-party supplier and customer liaison
    • Coordination of staff from multiple IT disciplines where necessary to resolve incidents
    • Documenting faults and recommending improvements
  • Service Management
    • Assist in service development, evaluation, and implementation within ITIL framework and corporate policy
    • Informing service managers on possible service developments and improvements
  • Supporting and maintaining the corporate integration platform
    • Maintain the integration platform and associated technologies to the levels set out in the IT SLA
    • Manage 3rd level integration support incidents
    • Assist with upgrades and testing, maintenance and improvement of the integration platform
    • Implement improvements to support efficiency and ‘business as usual’ processes associated with the team
  • Developing and implementing new services
    • Providing input into the technical design to ensure production of applications that deliver the needs of the business
    • Assisting with implementation and supporting growth of data engineering functions integrated with the Boomi platform
    • Liaise with colleagues to develop new processes in alignment with project specifications and agreed technical and security standards
    • Take ownership of designated development tasks and responsibility for their delivery
    • Deliver aspects of a project’s technical requirements – analysis, design, security, estimating, testing and implementation
    • Create process support, management and handover documentation
    • Investigate ideas and explore technical solutions to support development tasks
  • Maintain a considered, describable approach to workload prioritisation and time management in the context of a busy multi- channel environment

Corporate and statutory initiatives

  • Maintain up to date knowledge of corporate and departmental policies, procedures, and current systems to support compliance within the team

Working conditions

  • The team currently works to a hybrid model with a mixture of working from home and in the office. There's a minimum requirement to be office based one day per week and you'll need to attend meetings in person in Winchester when required
  • Display screen equipment user/laptop will be supplied
  • Successful completion of a Criminal Records Check and Non-Police Personnel Vetting will be required
